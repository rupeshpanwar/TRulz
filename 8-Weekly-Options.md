  - 2 spreads
  - directional neutral strategy but purely time decay
  - look for delta 20
  - suppose Apple is trading at 560 then
    - PUT Sell
     - Sell June 530 PUT
     - Buy June 520 PUT
   - CALL Sell
    - Sell June 590 CALL
    - Buy June 600 CALL
  - Adjust the strategy , in case stock goes down then buy a put of next month
